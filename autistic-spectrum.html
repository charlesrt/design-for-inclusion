Design for users on the Autistic spectrum

Autism is a lifelong developmental disability that affects how people perceive the world and interact with others. Autism is a spectrum condition. All autistic people share certain difficulties, but being autistic will affect them in different ways.
— The National Autistic Society

Do

Use simple colours

Don't

Use bright contrasting colours

Do

Write in plain language

Don't

Use figures of speech and idioms

Do

Use simple sentences and bullets

Don't

Create a wall of text

Do

Make buttons descriptive — for example, Attach files

Don't

Make buttons vague and unpredictable — for example, Click here

Do

Build simple and consistent layouts

Don't

Build complex and cluttered layouts
